Progressive Enhancement Writing Assignment

Progressive enhancment mainly focuses on the content. To get in that mindset we have to think about the content first and then base all other aspects on the content we chose. It's important in momdern web development because it ensures that your website is usable by people with older browsers, slower internet connections, or disabilities. It also helps your site perform well on all devices by loading only the necessary resources and etc. Progressive Enhancment
